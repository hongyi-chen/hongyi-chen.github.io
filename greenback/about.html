meet greenback.





We're a JA Central Ontario Company led by a group of high school students dedicated to constantly questioning, tinkering, and challenging to unlock great creativity. Our team never settles for anything less than greatness and always pushes for more.

We realized the many problems of wallets today: they're big and bulky, they're environmentally costly, and they're just straight up inconvenient. So four months ago, we set out on a mission - make a revolutionary wallet defying the norms of society. And here it is.

give back with greenback.

At greenback, we reconize the need for giving back to our community. Through a variety of initiatives from local tree planting to speaking on behalf of youth entrepreneurship with environmental organization Global Figure, greenback aims to create the change the world needs. One wallet at a time.
